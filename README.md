GEI / Auto Melody & Harp
Overview

GEI / Auto Melody & Harp is a lightweight mod designed to assist with melody and harp-style mini-games. It automatically detects note patterns and plays them with accurate timing, making music-based challenges easier and more consistent.

The mod helps reduce mistakes during fast note sequences and allows players to focus more on gameplay instead of memorizing long melodies.

Features

Automatic melody detection

Accurate harp note timing

Lightweight and simple to use

Improves consistency in music mini-games

Installation

Download the mod .jar file.

Place it into your game's mods folder.

Launch the game with your mod loader installed.

Usage

Once installed, the mod will automatically assist during melody or harp-based mini-games by detecting patterns and replaying them with precise timing.
